The App

My Pocket — Budget tracker

My Pocket

Free Android No account needed English & French

My Pocket helps you take control of your monthly finances using the envelope budgeting method. Create spending categories, track every expense, and visualize where your money goes — without ever creating an account or sharing your data.

View on Google Play

Envelope budgeting — done simply

Allocate your income into custom spending categories (envelopes). Enable or disable them each month. See exactly how much is left in each one at a glance.

Multiple projects

Manage separate budgets for different contexts — personal, family, travel — all in one app.

Monthly statistics

Charts and breakdowns showing your real spending versus your planned budget, per category.

Export as PDF or Excel

Generate a clean monthly report to share with your partner or keep as a record.

Scheduled reminders

Set daily, weekly or monthly notifications to keep your budget up to date.

100% offline & private

All data stays on your device. No account, no sync, no server. Your finances are yours alone.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This privacy policy applies to the My Pocket mobile application (the "App") published by an independent developer. By using the App, you agree to this policy.

Data we collect

My Pocket does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All financial data you enter (budgets, expenses, projects) is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. We do not have access to any of this data.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays banner advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain data to serve relevant ads, including device identifiers and usage data. This collection is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time through your device's ad settings (Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization).

Permissions

The App may request permission to send notifications (for budget reminders). This permission is optional and can be revoked at any time through your device settings. No other sensitive permissions are requested.

Third-party services

The App uses Google AdMob for advertising. No other third-party analytics, tracking, or data collection services are integrated.

Children's privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
